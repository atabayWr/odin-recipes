Dal Makhani

Description

Dal Makhani is a delicious, rich and creamy dish made from black lentils, spices, butter and cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 cup kidney beans(washed and soaked overnight)
  • 3 cups whole black gram (washed and soaked overnight)
  • 1/4 cup chana dal (washed and soaked overnight)
  • 1/3 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp fresh cream
  • 5-6 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, cubed
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a pressure cooker, add ghee, once it’s hot, add kidney beans, whole black urad dal, chana dal.
  2. Add water as required, garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder.
  3. Add salt to taste, unsalted butter and mix everything well.
  4. Cover it with the lid and cook on medium flame for 4 to 5 whistles or until the dal is 80 % cooked.
  5. In a heavy bottom pot, add ghee. Once it’s hot, add prepared ginger garlic paste and sauté it well. To this add red chili powder.
  6. Add fresh tomato puree, salt to taste and sauté well on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
  7. Add boiled dal, cover it with the lid and cook for 5-10 minutes on medium flame. Stir in between.
  8. Transfer it into a serving bowl, garnish it with fresh cream and serve hot with steamed rice.
